How to Use SwapReads

Step 1: Sign Up

To get started, click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in your name, email, and password. Once you've registered, check your email for a confirmation link and log in to your new account.

Tip: Use a strong password to secure your account!

Step 2: Browse Books

Once you're logged in, you can start browsing for books. Use the search bar to find books by title or author, or explore categories for inspiration. Click on any book to view its details and availability.

Tip: If you're not sure what to search for, check out the 'Popular Books' section on the homepage!

Step 3: Add a Book

Step 4: Start Swapping

When you find a book you want to swap, click on the "Request Swap" button. Once the other user accepts, you can arrange the details for the swap, like shipping or meet-up location.

Tip: Always check the book's condition before completing a swap to ensure it meets your expectations!

Step 5: Manage Your Account

In your account settings, you can update your profile, track swap requests, and manage your book listings. Make sure to keep your contact information up-to-date for a smooth swapping experience.

Tip: Check your swap history to see past swaps and feedback from other users!
